Pre-Launch Survey

When your product is up for launch, it is wise to hear an unbiased opinion that shared by the actual end-users to get their reactions to price, features, usability, and overall design. At this stage, it is important to identify any unexpected defects of the product, so that they can be corrected or mitigated in communication, packaging, or other elements of your marketing strategy. End-user reactions will also tell you which benefits are most important to emphasize in marketing communications.
